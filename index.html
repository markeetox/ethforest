Pastel Persons

Pastel Persons is a women-led NFT project made up of 8,900 randomly generated persons. Our mission is to onboard women, youth, families and other under-represented demographics into Web3 and provide educational resources for our community. Pastel Persons is dedicated to creating a community that leads with accessibility, inclusiveness, diversity and representation.
